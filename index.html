Usage

Whole web page consist of three panels, left and right sidebars, and panel with a photo.Left sidebar consists of buttons with different functionality.You can edit, reset, save the image, and also upload from the device or by URL.On the right sidebar, you can enter the photo that you want to edit, and see it in slider below. Or those default.When you click "Edit Image", you will see a pop-up window. There you can do with the image all that you want.
